About ShipDecKK

Excellence in maritime dealership since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK emerged from a passionate vision to revolutionize the maritime industry. What started as a small team of maritime enthusiasts has grown into a leading ship dealership, specializing in high-end yachts, cargo ships, and luxury liners.

Our rapid rise to prominence in the maritime industry is a testament to our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We recognized a gap in the market for a dealership that combined exceptional vessels with unparalleled customer service, and we set out to fill that void.

Today, ShipDecKK stands as a trusted name in the maritime industry, known for our carefully curated selection of vessels and our dedication to helping clients find their perfect ship. Our reputation has been built on the foundation of quality, reliability, and the highest standards of customer care.

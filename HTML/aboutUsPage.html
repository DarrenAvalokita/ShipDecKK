About ShipDecKK

Excellence in maritime dealership since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK began with a vision to redefine excellence in the maritime world. What started as a bold idea quickly evolved into a trusted name in global ship dealership, known for high standards and exceptional service.

We specialize in high-end yachts, robust cargo ships, and luxury liners—each crafted with precision, using only premium materials to ensure durability, performance, and elegance. Whether it’s for leisure or commercial use, our curated fleet meets a wide range of needs.

At ShipDecKK, we go beyond selling ships. Our personalized approach, attention to detail, and commitment to customer satisfaction have earned us a reputation for reliability and excellence. We’re here to help you sail with confidence.
